Famous World Monuments

Explore some of the most famous monuments around the world.

Eiffel Tower

Located in France, the Eiffel Tower is one of the most iconic structures in the world.

Statue of Liberty

Located in the USA, the Statue of Liberty is a symbol of freedom and democracy.

Taj Mahal

Located in India, the Taj Mahal is a beautiful white marble mausoleum.

Great Wall of China

Located in China, the Great Wall is a historic fortification built to protect ancient China.
